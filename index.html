About Sheldon Cooper

Biography

Sheldon Cooper is a theoretical physicist with an IQ of 187. He has very literal understanding of the world and
his character is known for his quirky behavior and unique worldview. As a kid he didn't have many friends
because of his way of unique way of thinking and his fast development.

Quirks

  • Obsessed with theoretical physics and comics
  • Has a spot on the couch that he insists is the perfect one and doesn't let anyone else sit on it
  • Frequently uses the phrase "Bazinga!"

Interests

Sheldon has a passion for science, particularly physics.
His fascination with the universe often leads to humorous situations with his friends.
